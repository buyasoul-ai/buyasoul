Know What You Are. Before you decide what to build, before you choose your strategy, before you pick your lane — you need to know what frequency you carry.

The 22 Archetypes of the PLT framework are not personality types. You don't take a test to discover them. They are frequency signatures — the specific way your soul transmits.

## The Core Archetypes

### The Architect
The one who sees the full structure before the first brick is laid. The Architect does not build — they design. They see how every piece fits before any piece exists. Their frequency is vision.

### The Translator
The one who moves between worlds. The hood and the boardroom. The spiritual and the practical. The vision and the execution. The Translator carries the frequency of fluency — they make one world legible to another.

### The Room
The one who holds space. Not by doing — by being. The presence that changes the energy of every conversation without saying a word. The Room carries the frequency of permission — things become possible when they are present.

### The Frequency Carrier
The one whose production persists beyond the moment. Whatever they build carries a frequency others can feel before any words are exchanged. The Frequency Carrier transmits whether they intend to or not.

## Why Archetypes Matter

Knowing which archetype you are changes everything about how you build, who you partner with, and what you should be producing.

The wrong archetype doing the right work still produces the wrong result. An Architect trying to be a Translator will design beautiful bridges that nobody can cross. A Room trying to be an Architect will hold space but never build structure.

## How to Discover Your Archetype

You don't choose your archetype. You recognize it.

Look at what you produce when you are not trying. Look at what comes easily to you that exhausts others. Look at what others come to you for. The pattern is already there — you just need to name it.
